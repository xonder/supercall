# Privacy Policy

**Supercall by Xonder**
**Last Updated: February 15, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Supercall ("we", "our", or "the Service"), an AI-powered communication assistant built on the OpenClaw platform, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our SMS messaging service.

## Information We Collect

When you use Supercall, we may collect the following information:

- **Phone Number**: Your mobile phone number, provided by you during account setup, used to send and receive text messages.
- **Message Content**: The content of text messages sent to and from the Service for the purpose of delivering AI assistant functionality (reminders, notifications, weather updates, and other informational alerts).
- **Usage Data**: Basic usage information such as message timestamps and delivery status, used to ensure reliable message delivery.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

- To deliver AI-powered text message notifications, reminders, and informational alerts that you have requested.
- To process and respond to your messages.
- To maintain and improve the reliability of the Service.

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.**

We may share information only with the following service providers, strictly for the purpose of delivering our Service:

- **Twilio**: Our SMS delivery provider, which processes your phone number and message content to deliver text messages. Twilio's privacy policy is available at [twilio.com/legal/privacy](https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy).
- **OpenAI**: Our AI provider, which may process message content to generate AI responses. OpenAI's privacy policy is available at [openai.com/privacy](https://openai.com/privacy).

## Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Request deletion** of your personal data.
- **Opt out** of receiving text messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message.
- **Request help** by replying **HELP** to any message.

## Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue at [github.com/xonder/supercall/issues](https://github.com/xonder/supercall/issues).
